Welcome!

My name is Saffat Aziz and I am self taught freelance photographer/cinematography based out of Ottawa, Ontario. I do all kinds of photography ranging from lanscape, portraits, food, all the way to real estate photography. My camera gear includes a canon 70D as my main camera and for the lenses I juggle between Signma 17-50, canon 50 prime, tokina 6.5 mm fisheye. Over the years I have worked for a variety of organizations doing photoshoots, shooting promo videos for clubs , music videos for local bands and much more. . I am currently persuing a Bachelor's degree in computer engineering and business management at the University of Ottawa. Alongside engineering, I spend my free time being a fulltime freelance Photographer. This website serves as a means to display some of my artwork. Scroll down to find out more.
